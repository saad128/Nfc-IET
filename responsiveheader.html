VC Message



National Fertilizer Corporation of Paistan is one of the largest employers of qualified engineers particularly in the field of Chemical Engineering.In order to meet the requirement of trained manpower for chemical process industry especially in the ferilizer sector,a Training Center was established in 1985 with the technical assistance of M/s. COSMO international Co., Ltd., Japan. The Center trained managers, engineers To further diversify the activities of the Center and in line with the education policy announced by the Government of Pakistan, NFC with the approval of Ministry of Industries & Production, Government of Pakistan upgrade the Centre in 1994 and started graduate engineering classes initially in Chemical Engineering discipline and changed its nomenclature as "NFC" Institute of Engineering & Technological training". This was also the need of the country and particularly of this area.
